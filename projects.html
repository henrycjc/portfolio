History Chains is a online social / collaboration network designed to help high school students studying history complete research and act as a study guide. It was designed and developed by myself and four other students at The University of Queensland for DECO1800 "Design Computing Studio 1".


You can view history chains online at deco1800-01.zones.net or by cloning yourself a copy via our Git repository.

You can view a copy of the major project via Blackboard.

My contributions included most of the programming related queries including discussing the GitHub page, the document structure and all other related development queries throughout the documents.

You are looking at it! This portfolio was designed using Twitter Bootstrap but, just for Lorna I took extra special care to make sure it didn't look like Bootstrap.
